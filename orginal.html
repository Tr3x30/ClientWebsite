About Team 9062

Team 9062 was created with a simple but meaningful goal: to inspire students to explore the world of robotics and programming. In our team, students are able to learn the fundamentals of coding, robot design, and engineering thinking. Members work together to solve problems, develop creative ideas, and turn those ideas into real robotic systems. Beyond technical skills, Team 9062 is also a place where students build friendships, develop communication skills, and learn the importance of teamwork. We believe that innovation grows from collaboration, curiosity, and the courage to try again after failure. Our mission is to provide a supportive environment where students can experiment, learn from mistakes, and grow into confident creators of technology.
