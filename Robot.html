Meet, RIPTIDE

RIPTIDE was our robot for the 2025 REEFSCAPE game. REEFSCAPE required robots to score coral (large PVC pipe) onto a multilayered reef to score points. Addditionaly, robots had to remove algae (large bouncy balls) and clean the reef to allow space for coral to be scored. Finally robots could attatch the the barge at the end of the game to escape the depths of the water and gain more points!

Drivetrain

Featuring swerve motors, RIPTIDE was able to maneveure around the field easily, allowing for flexibility when playing offence or defences-whatever the alliance needed most.

Elevator

RIPTIDE's elevator used precise motors to allow the robot to score on 3 of 4 levels of the reef, quickly racking up points while staying out of the way of robots that were specialized for scoring on the top level.

Climber

The climber attached to RIPTIDE was designed to allow the robot to connect to a deep cage, providing the opportunity to score 12 of 14 reqiured BARGE points, meaning that other teammates would only need to park under the barge to achieve the coveted ranking point!
